Pipeline

How BrailleVision Works

Four intelligent steps transform a camera frame into spoken language in real time.

01
Camera Capture

High-resolution 1280×720 live feed captured via WebRTC, streamed as base64 frames over WebSocket at 2 FPS.

02
Preprocessing

OpenCV CLAHE enhances contrast and adaptive thresholding isolates Braille dots even in poor lighting conditions.

03
AI Detection

YOLOv8 nano model detects and classifies individual Braille cells with sub-100ms inference time on CPU.

04
Speech Output

Decoded characters are assembled into words and converted to natural speech via the on-device TTS engine.
