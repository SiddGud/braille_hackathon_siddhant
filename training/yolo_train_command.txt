# Model Training Information

> **IMPORTANT:** This project uses a **pre-trained YOLOv8 Braille Detection model** downloaded from Roboflow Universe.
> No custom training was performed during the hackathon. The training notebook (`braille_train.ipynb`) is provided
> to show how a similar model can be reproduced from scratch if needed.
>
> The primary engineering contribution of this submission is the full-stack application:
> real-time WebSocket inference, heuristic text repair, voice commands, and the TTS pipeline.

## Hyperparameters
- **Base Model:** yolov8n.pt (YOLOv8 Nano)
- **Epochs:** 100
- **Image Size:** 640
- **Batch Size:** 16
- **Optimizer:** Auto (AdamW)
- **Learning Rate:** Auto (0.01 initial)

## Training Results
Training results, confusion matrix, and metric plots are stored in `training/results/`.
Please see `results/` folder for:
- `results.png` — mAP50 and mAP50-95 curves
- `confusion_matrix.png`
- `val_batch0_pred.jpg` — sample validation predictions
